My Hobbies:


Martial Arts

I have been doing Martial Arts for over 10 years now. This includes Kick-boxing, Karate, BJJ, and Kung-fu. Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is a new hobby of mine. I first tried it when I was invited to a pilot cybersecurity class in my high school senior year, and almost immediately I was completely taken over by the desire to learn more about it, in fact, the only reason I am majoring in Computer Science is to get into a Cybersecurity program in FAU. By now I have completed over a dozen courses and am preparing for a major certification in this field. In my opinion, Udemy is one of the best websites where you can go to learn about cybersecurity and a lot of other stuff, many professionals post their courses there for very cheap prices.


Rock Climbing

It has only been a few months since I started doing Rock Climbing and I have to say that it is definitely one of the best things I have ever tried out. The sense of achievement every time I make that hard jump or reach the top of the trail is simply overwhelming. Project ROCK is an extremely good gym for rock climbing with friendly staff and supportive people, people of all levels will find that place to be really good.


Sky Diving

Skydiving is probably the riskiest activity I have been doing so far. I have only jumped from the plane once, however, I frequently go to do indoor skydiving which lets you experience freefall and learn how to control your body to the fullest. IFLY offers an indoor skydiving experience and training for people of all levels, and although it is quite expansive, it is definitely worth it to go there at least once.


Snowboarding

Snowboarding is that hobby that I come back to every year. Sadly, I can't snowboard all the time so I have to wait up until winter before I can go and do it again. I have been snowboarding for 6 years now and am pretty proficient in it, although I cannot do any advanced tricks on a board yet. Every winter, I usually go to the Beech Mountain Resort to snowboard, and I have to say that although it is not as fancy as many other places, it is also extremely cheap in comparison to other famous resorts and is a good place for newcomers to try things out.
